About

Hi there!

Sjaan (Jeanne Shaw) is an Experience Designer, Artist and Creative Producer. Developing and organising immersive experiences, interactive installations and art exhibitions. Her work centers on the compelling fusion of audiovisual technology and physical space. With a diverse background in graphic design, 3D design and styling, Sjaan is now mainly focused on using interactive technology as her artistic medium. Sjaan is born in 1997 and grew up always interested in computers and devices. Now, her artistic research delves into natural phenomena and the human (dis)connection to nature and technology. By employing live-generated data and audience inputs, she crafts dynamic experiences. Translating unseen forces and real-time interactions into new aesthetic insights, revealing patterns and connections we might not have consciously perceived. Her work can been described as a blend of art, design and technology, with a focus on creating immersive and interactive experiences. Sjaan is passionate about exploring the potential of technology to create new forms of artistic expression and to connect people with nature in innovative and playfyul ways.

Background
Sjaan has always been drawing, painting and making collages. In 2017 she graduated in Spatial and Visual design at the Nimeto Academy in Utrecht. After that, she did her Bachelor of Design at the interdisciplinary Art Academy Fontys School of Fine and Performing Artsin Tilburg. Graduated in 2022.

Since 2019 she works as allround designer on various art projects.
With her studio she likes to connect people. She organizes art exhibitions, workshops (for kids), cross-overs and other creative experiences.
